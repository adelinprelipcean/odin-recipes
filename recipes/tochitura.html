Tochitura

Very popular, the tochitura is a traditional Romanian dish, made of pork meat and vegetables.

Ingredients:
  • 300g pork meat
  • 200g pork sausage
  • 100g chicken livers
  • 200g sliced mushrooms
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 150 ml tomato puree
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Oil (but no olive oil)
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Paprika
  • 4 eggs
  • Polenta
  • 150g of Telemea cheese
  • Salt,pepper
Steps:
  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan and add the minced garlic cloves. Brown the garlic for 2 or 3 minutes, until the oil takes on their aroma.
  2. Take them out and add the finely chopped onions, the pork meat cut in pieces and over high heat, brown each side.
  3. Then add the sausage cut in pieces and a little of water.
  4. Cover the saucepan and let it fry at a medium heat for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the chicken livers, cut in pieces, diced peeled tomatoes, the tomato puree and simmer.
  6. Then, add the chopped thyme, the rosemary, paprika, salt and pepper and let stand for 3 minutes.
  7. In parallel, prepare the polenta. Follow the instructions on the package.
  8. Use approximately 1/2 liter of water, salt and pour in the polenta, stirring with a whisk. Let it boil during 15-20 minutes.
  9. To finish, take the eggs and cook them in a pan with a little oil. Fried egg type.
  10. Serve everything on a plate: polenta, meat, on top the fried egg and sprinkle with cheese.
  11. Little extra: this dish can be served with pickles and/or feta.
